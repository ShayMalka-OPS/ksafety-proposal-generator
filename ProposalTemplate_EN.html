🛡 What is K-Safety?

K-Safety is an intelligent Command & Control platform designed to unify all security systems at a facility under one roof – CCTV cameras, face and license plate recognition, AI-based Video Analytics, panic buttons, alarm systems (PIMA, RISCO), and access control (LENEL and others). The system manages events in real time, executes smart BPM workflows, and presents a complete operational picture on an interactive GIS map.

⚡ Core Capabilities
🎥

Unified Video Management

Connect all CCTV, LPR, and face recognition cameras. Live view, archive, and smart search.

🤖

Video Analytics (AI)

Intrusion detection, abandoned objects, people counting, virtual lines – all in real time.

👤

Face & License Plate Recognition

Instant alerts for people or vehicles on blacklists/whitelists via a central interface.

🚨

Smart Event Management

Automated BPM/RBE: every event receives a workflow, owner, SLA, and tracking until closure.

🗺

GIS / Live Map

All cameras, sensors, and forces marked on an interactive real-time map.

📱

K-React – Field App

Field forces receive tasks, navigation, and situational awareness directly on mobile.

📊

BI & Reports

Real-time dashboards, executive reports, trend analysis, and custom KPIs.

🔔

Panic Buttons / IoT

Connect to SOS buttons, gas detectors, environmental sensors – BACnet, Modbus, MQTT.

👥

Shift & Force Management

Shift planning, status updates, communication, and force coordination in real time.
