# K-Safety Proposal Generator — Release History

## v1.0 — 2026-04-08

**Initial Launch**

The K-Safety Proposal Generator is now live for the Kabatone sales team. This is the first full release of the tool.

**What you can do:**

- **Build a proposal in minutes** — A step-by-step wizard walks you through selecting the right K-Safety modules for your customer. No technical knowledge required.
- **Annual vs. Perpetual pricing, side by side** — The tool automatically calculates both pricing models, including a 5-year cost comparison, so you can show customers exactly what each option costs over time.
- **Prices you can adjust** — All prices start from the official Kabatone price list, but you can edit them on the fly if a deal requires custom pricing.
- **Cloud or On-Prem** — Choose the deployment model your customer needs; the proposal adjusts accordingly.
- **Infrastructure requirements calculated automatically** — Based on the modules and quantities you select, the tool works out the server and hardware requirements with no manual counting.
- **AI-written executive summary** — Click one button and the tool generates a customer-specific executive summary for the proposal, tailored to the city and modules selected.
- **Export to PDF** — Generate a polished, print-ready PDF proposal with the Kabatone logo and full brand styling.
- **Export to Word** — Download a fully editable Word document so you can make final tweaks before sending.
- **Proposal history** — Every proposal you generate is saved automatically. Come back any time to view, reuse, or delete past proposals.
- **Kabatone brand styling** — Proposals are formatted with the official Kabatone colors, logo, and typography — ready to send to any customer.
